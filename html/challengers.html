CHALLENGES

REDUCE YOUR ENERGY CONSUMPTION BY 20% FOR A WEEK
This challenge consists of consciously reducing energy consumption in your home. Turn off lights when not in a room, unplug unused electronic devices, and consider using low-consumption bulbs. Record your efforts and the results achieved.
Zero Waste: A Day Without Trash.
In this challenge, your goal is to spend an entire day producing as little waste as possible. Refuse disposable items, bring your own container for meals, and avoid unnecessary packaging. Take photos or note your experiences throughout the day.
Plant a Tree: Growing Together for a Healthy Planet.
Do your part for the environment by planting a tree. Research native trees in your area, choose a suitable location, and plant a tree in your yard, a local park, or a designated area. Track its growth over the months.
Sustainable Transport Week: Leave the Car at Home!
In this challenge, you commit to not using the car for an entire week. Instead, opt for more sustainable transportation options such as biking, public transport, walking, or carpooling. Record the distance traveled, fuel savings, and reduction in carbon emissions. Share your experiences and challenges throughout the week.

About Us

The GreenLife Tracker is a platform dedicated to inspiring and guiding people in their journeys towards a more sustainable and environmentally conscious lifestyle. Our goal is to provide resources, challenges, and a vibrant community to support the adoption of more eco-friendly practices in daily life.
